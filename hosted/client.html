Othello

Introduction

Welcome to my implementation of Othello. This was a fun game that I used to play with my cousins that I wished to bring back on the colored screen. To Start playing, you need another player to connect

How to Play

  1. When its your turn, place your color piece so that it flanks the other pieces, a predict circle will help you see a possible move
  2. The game is finished when the whole board is filled with pieces
  3. Note: You may play this game where their are no possible moves for a player, yet spaces are still open. This is still a bug I have to fix
